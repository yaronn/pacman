GifW00t! is a pure-javascript web recorder.
1. Click the record button (on top)
2. Click anywhere in the play surface to start a game
3. After your game click the stop button

When the clip is ready click play to see it. In your first time, record just a few seconds so rendering is faster.

You can use the GifW00t! recorder in any site, even sites written by other people!
