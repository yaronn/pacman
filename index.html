Gifw00t! Pacman

GifW00t! is a pure-javascript web recorder. Record any HTML, any site!
1. Click the record button (on top left)
2. Click on the game surface to start a game
3. Play a few seconds and click the stop button
4. When the clip is ready click play to see it

More Examples: Helicopter, Paint, Editor
